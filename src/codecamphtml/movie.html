The Nut Job

Animated film about Surly, an exiled, selfish squirrel who, along with his human-buddied rat companion Buddy, discovers a nut store filled with food. He makes a deal with his former park community, who are struggling to survive the winter, to help them steal the nuts from the store. However, Surly's true intentions are to keep the food for himself, while the store is secretly a front for a human bank robbery, leading to a chaotic heist where Surly must eventually learn the value of teamwork and loyalty.
